What you get

The basics, done properly.

Your files, your server

Upload files, organize them into folders, and preview images and PDFs in the browser. Deleted items sit in trash until you say otherwise.

Private share links

Share a file with a link. Set an expiry date if you want one, and revoke any link whenever you like.

Admin controls

Invite users with single-use codes, set per-user storage limits, and promote or disable accounts from the admin panel.

Runs on your hardware

One Docker Compose file. Runs happily on a Raspberry Pi, a NAS, or whatever old machine you have lying around.

Own your cloud. Start in minutes.

Your files never leave your server. No subscriptions, no trackers.
